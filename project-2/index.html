How to perform STANDUP COMEDY




Welcome to an introductory website into the world of standup comedy.
We've all heard the saying "laughter is the best medicine" but most of us have never given it much thought beyond that.
Comedians, as a group, are believed to be one of the most depressed and psychologically darker groups in society.
The stage is where they can openly release what they are feeling in a healthy manner, and in doing so,
aleviate some of the stress in their lives.
The phyical act of laughing is proven to reduce stress, raise lower the risk of heart disease, and
create the production and release of endorphins, which is a chemical in the body that makes us "feel good".
So in a way, comedians help heal society while healing themselves.
The following pages will illustrate why and how comedians do what they do.
